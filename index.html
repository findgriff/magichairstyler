Frequently asked questions

Still deciding? Here are the things customers ask us most. Email magichairstyler@aressentinel.com if we missed yours.

Will it work on my hair type?
Yes. The AirStyler has three heat settings (160°C, 180°C, 200°C) designed for fine, normal and thick hair. The ionic and infrared system is also gentle enough for colour-treated and chemically relaxed hair.
Can I use it on wet or damp hair?
Use the AirStyler on towel-dried, damp-to-dry hair for best results. For curls and waves, hair should be almost fully dry. Do not use on soaking-wet hair.
Will it damage my hair?
The tourmaline-ceramic plates distribute heat evenly (preventing hot spots) and the ionic technology actively conditions the cuticle while you style. Used at the correct heat setting, daily use is safe.
How long does delivery take?
Orders ship within 24 hours on business days. Typical delivery is 3–6 business days in the UK and EU, and 5–10 business days for the rest of the world. Free worldwide — no thresholds, no hidden fees.
What countries do you ship to?
We ship worldwide from our UK warehouse. Your plug adapter (UK / EU / US) is selected automatically at checkout based on your shipping country.
Is the plug type compatible with my country?
Yes. The AirStyler is dual voltage (100–240V) and we ship the correct plug type for your destination. It also works with a standard travel adaptor abroad.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely happy, contact us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be returned in original condition. Full detail in the Refund Policy below.
Is the checkout secure?
Checkout is handled by Stripe, the same payment processor used by Apple, Amazon, Shopify and hundreds of thousands of businesses. Your card never touches our servers.
Is there a warranty?
Yes — every AirStyler comes with a 12-month manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Contact us with your order number to file a claim.
How is this different from the £400+ premium brands?
Under the shell, the core hardware (tourmaline-ceramic plates, ionic + infrared heat, dual voltage) is the same technology the big brands use. We skip the retail markup and ship direct, which is why we can offer the same result at £49.99.
